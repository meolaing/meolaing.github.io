The Godfrey Hotel

http://www.godfreyhotelboston.com
505 Washington Street
Boston, MA 02111

A brand-new hotel close to the Downtown Crossing T stop, The Godfrey is in the center of downtown Boston. It is near Boston Common and the Public Garden, Chinatown, the Theater District, and Back Bay. We have a courtesy room block available at the Godfrey for $329.00 a night under Ing/Meola Wedding Block; parking is valet-only and an extra $52.00 a day including in/out privileges. This rate is available only until 7/22/16 (any unbooked rooms will be released back into their available pool at the standard rate 8 weeks before the wedding); please book early if you are planning on attending.
